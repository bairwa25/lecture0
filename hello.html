White Sauce Pasta Recipe

About White Sauce Pasta Recipe:

Pasta originally originated from traditional Italian cuisine, you can make pasta in different like white sauce and tomato sauce. Today we are going to make a White cheese pasta recipe or cheesy white pasta.

The white cheese pasta recipe is typically in many shapes like a noodle made from an unleavened dough of a durum wheat flour mixed with water or eggs and formed into sheets or various shapes, then cooked by boiling. It can also be made with flour from other cereals or grains. Pasta may be divided into two broad categories, dried (pasta secca) and fresh (pasta fresca). So lets make white pasta recipe step-by-step.

Ingredients needed to make White sauce pasta:

A) Main Ingredients :

  • 2 cups of shell pasta
  • 4 tbsp - processed cheese
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Chili flakes
  • Pasta seasoning

B) For White Sauce:

  • 50 gms butter
  • 1/2 spoon plain flour
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper - Grounded
  • 1/2 liter - milk
  • According to the taste - salt
